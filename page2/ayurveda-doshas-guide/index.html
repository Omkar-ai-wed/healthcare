Vata - The Energy of Movement

Air + Space (Ether)

Vata governs all movement in the body and mind, from the circulation of blood to the flow of thoughts.

Primary Functions

  • Nervous System: Controls all nerve impulses and brain function
  • Circulation: Governs blood flow and lymphatic drainage
  • Breathing: Manages respiratory function and oxygen distribution
  • Elimination: Controls bowel movements, urination, and waste removal
  • Movement: Responsible for all bodily movements and joint function

Physical Characteristics

Build: Naturally thin, light frame, prominent bones
Weight: Difficulty gaining weight, tends to be underweight
Skin: Dry, rough, cool to touch, prone to wrinkles
Hair: Dry, frizzy, coarse, prone to split ends
Eyes: Small, dark, active, darting movement
Appetite: Variable, irregular, often forgets to eat

Mental & Emotional Qualities

Positive Traits
Creative Enthusiastic Flexible Quick thinking Artistic
Imbalance Traits
Anxious Worried Scattered Indecisive Fearful

When Balanced

  • Creative and artistic expression flows naturally
  • Mental alertness and quick comprehension
  • Regular elimination and healthy circulation
  • Enthusiasm and joy in daily activities
  • Flexible and adaptable to change

When Imbalanced

Physical:
Constipation, Dry skin, Joint pain, Headaches, Insomnia
Mental:
Anxiety, Worry, Racing thoughts, Difficulty concentrating
Emotional:
Fear, Nervousness, Feeling scattered or overwhelmed

Balancing Recommendations

Diet: Warm, moist, grounding foods; regular meal times; avoid raw/cold foods
Lifestyle: Regular routine, adequate rest, gentle exercise, oil massage
Environment: Warm, stable, calm surroundings; avoid excessive stimulation
üî•

Pitta - The Energy of Transformation

Fire + Water

Pitta governs all metabolic processes and transformations in the body and mind.

Primary Functions

  • Metabolism: Controls all digestive and metabolic processes
  • Body Temperature: Regulates heat production and temperature control
  • Vision: Governs eyesight and visual perception
  • Intelligence: Manages comprehension, analysis, and discrimination
  • Skin Health: Controls complexion, skin color, and texture

Physical Characteristics

Build: Medium build, well-proportioned, muscular
Weight: Moderate weight, gains/loses weight relatively easily
Skin: Soft, warm, fair, prone to freckles, moles, rashes
Hair: Fine, soft, early graying or balding, often blonde/red
Eyes: Medium size, penetrating gaze, light colored
Appetite: Strong, regular, becomes irritable when hungry

Mental & Emotional Qualities

Positive Traits
Intelligent Focused Ambitious Confident Natural leaders
Imbalance Traits
Irritable Impatient Critical Aggressive Perfectionist

When Balanced

  • Sharp intellect and strong comprehension
  • Efficient digestion and robust appetite
  • Natural leadership and confidence
  • Healthy competitive spirit
  • Radiant complexion and strong immunity

When Imbalanced

Physical:
Acid reflux, Skin rashes, Excessive sweating, Inflammation
Mental:
Criticism, Impatience, Perfectionism, Workaholic tendencies
Emotional:
Anger, Irritability, Frustration, Judgmental attitude

Balancing Recommendations

Diet: Cool, sweet, bitter foods; avoid spicy, sour, salty foods; regular meals
Lifestyle: Moderate exercise, avoid overheating, stress management, cooling activities
Environment: Cool, calm settings; avoid excessive heat and competitive pressure
üåç

Kapha - The Energy of Structure

Earth + Water

Kapha provides structure, stability, and lubrication throughout the body and mind.

Primary Functions

  • Structure: Provides physical strength and structural integrity
  • Immunity: Maintains immune system and disease resistance
  • Lubrication: Keeps joints, tissues, and organs properly lubricated
  • Stability: Provides emotional stability and mental calmness
  • Growth: Controls growth and development throughout life

Physical Characteristics

Build: Large frame, solid build, naturally strong
Weight: Tends to be overweight, gains weight easily, loses slowly
Skin: Thick, soft, smooth, cool, pale, well-hydrated
Hair: Thick, wavy, lustrous, oily, dark colored
Eyes: Large, beautiful, calm expression, often blue or brown
Appetite: Slow, steady, can skip meals without discomfort

Mental & Emotional Qualities

Positive Traits
Calm Patient Loving Loyal Steady Reliable
Imbalance Traits
Stubborn Possessive Lethargic Resistant to change

When Balanced

  • Strong immunity and rarely gets sick
  • Loving, compassionate, and supportive nature
  • Steady energy and endurance
  • Excellent long-term memory
  • Natural strength and stability

When Imbalanced

Physical:
Weight gain, Congestion, Sluggish digestion, Water retention
Mental:
Mental fog, Depression, Lack of motivation, Dullness
Emotional:
Attachment, Possessiveness, Resistance to change, Greed

Balancing Recommendations

Diet: Light, warm, spicy foods; reduce dairy, sugar, heavy foods
Lifestyle: Vigorous exercise, stay active, avoid oversleeping, seek stimulation
Environment: Warm, dry, stimulating environments; embrace change and variety
