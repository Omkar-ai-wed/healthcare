Important Medical Disclaimers

Primary Disclaimer

Ayurvedic dosha assessment is for educational and wellness purposes only. Not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment.

Assessment Limitations

  • Results are educational estimates, not definitive diagnoses
  • Individual variation exists within constitutional types
  • Professional consultation recommended for personalized guidance
  • Cultural and genetic factors may influence constitutional expression
  • Snapshot assessment may not reflect life-long patterns

Medical Limitations

  • Not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment
  • Consult healthcare providers before making significant health changes
  • Do not discontinue prescribed medications without medical supervision
  • Seek immediate medical attention for serious health concerns
  • Online assessments provide general guidance only

Legal Responsibilities

  • Users responsible for their own health decisions
  • Information provided for educational purposes only
  • Professional medical advice takes precedence
  • Individual results may vary significantly
  • No guarantee of specific health outcomes

Medical Consultation Guidelines

When and how to coordinate with healthcare providers

Seek Immediate Medical Attention

Emergency Symptoms

  • Acute illness or sudden health changes
  • Severe symptoms (chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe pain)
  • Chronic conditions requiring ongoing medical management
  • Mental health crises or severe emotional disturbances
  • Pregnancy complications or concerning symptoms

Medication Interactions

  • Blood thinners (warfarin, heparin)
  • Diabetes medications

Herbal & Supplement Safety

Critical safety guidelines for herbs and natural supplements

General Herbal Safety Principles

Quality Sourcing

Use only reputable, tested herbal products from certified suppliers

  • Third-party testing for purity
  • Organic certification preferred
  • Proper botanical identification
  • Contamination screening

Dosage Guidelines

Follow recommended dosages carefully - more is not better

  • Start with lowest effective dose
  • Increase gradually if needed
  • Respect maximum daily limits
  • Consider body weight and age

Duration Limits

Avoid long-term use without professional supervision

  • Most herbs: 4-6 weeks maximum
  • Periodic breaks recommended
  • Professional guidance for longer use
  • Monitor for tolerance or dependence

Individual Reactions

Monitor for adverse effects and discontinue if problems occur

  • Start with small test doses
  • Watch for allergic reactions
  • Note any unusual symptoms
  • Stop immediately if adverse effects occur

Drug-Herb Interactions

Over 200 documented drug-herb interactions exist. Always consult healthcare providers before combining herbs with medications.

Blood Thinners (Warfarin, Heparin)

Avoid: Garlic, ginkgo, turmeric in large amounts
Monitor: INR levels more frequently
Action: Consult physician before adding any herbs

Diabetes Medications

Caution: Bitter melon, fenugreek, gymnema
Monitor: Blood glucose levels closely
Action: Medication doses may need adjustment

Blood Pressure Drugs

Avoid: High sodium herbs, excessive licorice
Monitor: Blood pressure regularly
Action: Coordinate with prescribing physician

Psychiatric Medications

Caution: Mood-affecting herbs like St. John's wort
Monitor: Changes in mental state
Action: Coordinate with mental health provider

Contraindications and Special Populations

Pregnancy & Breastfeeding

  • Only use pregnancy-safe herbs under supervision
  • First trimester particularly sensitive
  • Consult qualified prenatal practitioner
  • Avoid herbs that stimulate uterine contractions

Liver Disease

  • Monitor liver function tests
  • Use only under medical supervision
  • Avoid alcohol-based preparations
  • Watch for signs of liver stress

Kidney Disease

‚ö†Ô∏è Caution with diuretic herbs
  • Avoid nephrotoxic substances
  • Monitor kidney function
  • Adjust for decreased elimination
  • Careful with electrolyte balance

  • Inform surgeon of all herbal use
