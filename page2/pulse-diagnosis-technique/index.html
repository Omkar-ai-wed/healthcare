Pattern Recognition and Diagnosis Integration

Qi Deficiency Pattern

Pulse Characteristics: Weak, deep, slow or normal rate
Accompanying Symptoms: Fatigue, shortness of breath, poor appetite, pale complexion
Tongue Signs: Pale, swollen, with tooth marks

Blood Stasis Pattern

Pulse Characteristics: Choppy, hesitant, or wiry quality
Accompanying Symptoms: Fixed pain, dark complexion, purple lips
Tongue Signs: Purple tongue with purple spots
